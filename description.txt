Application name: Roman Numerals
Application description:
Roman Numerals provides an easy way of converting Roman numerals into more understandable numbers. Use it when you cannot quite work out the age of a building or to resolve deputes about the year in which a TV programme was made.

To use it just type in the roman numerals and the year in numbers will appear below.
